2 minute recap

Transfer your memories and belongings to your loved ones

1. Upload your content into capsules

Each capsule is composed of memories which you can upload or create directly in-app.

2. Assign people to each capsule

Add people from your contact list to each capsule. It is better to add as many contact points as possible.

3. Setup capsule conditions

At this point, there is nothing else to do. Once the conditions are met, your messages will be sent.
