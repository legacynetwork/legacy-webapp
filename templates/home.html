2 minute recap





Transfer your memories and belongings



1. Upload your content into capsules

Each capsule is composed of "memories" which are files you can upload or create directly in-app.



2. Assign people to the capsule

Add people from your contact list to every capsule. or redundancy, its better to add as many contact points as possible.



3. Setup capsule conditions

After setting conditions, there isn't much else to do. Once the conditions are met, the live-man's switch is activated and your capsules will be sent. They may also be updated at any point.

Ethereum technology



SEND ANYTHING

  • Memories such as letters or videos (IPFS)
  • Ether and ethereum tokens (ERC20 or others)
  • Digital smart-property of any object (according to the local laws)


SIMPLE

Setup your memory capsules and modify them in few, simple steps.
The dapp will be a responsive design accesible on web and mobile devices.



SAFE AND LONG LASTING

Built on top of the Ethereum blockchain, the service is guaranteed to last for many years.

A decentralized version with self-hosting files on multiple devices and locations is planned for a future version.
